Welcome

My name is Kevin Doyle. I am a graduate of Stony Brook University. In 2013, I completed my undergraduate degree in Biology and have returned to obtain my masters degree in the department of Ecology and Evolution which I plan to finish in the Fall of 2015. I have done research in Baines' Lab of Aquatic Ecology and continue to work there while I pursue my masters. Read more about the work I have done under the Projects section.

My current focus is on food quality regarding nutrient limitation. I am observing the effect of low iron on the copepod Acartia tonsa. Specifically, I will be determining the specific concentration of iron where these copepods begin to show signs of limitation.

This new site is currently under construction and will be for some time, so please bear with me during this arduous transition.

I have also lately developed an interest in Evolution and Genomics and will be pursuing opportunities in these areas as well.
