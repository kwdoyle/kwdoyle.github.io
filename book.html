The Crude War


By the way, I wrote this book for NaNoWriMo. Click on the book to purchase.

Description

Oil is in scarce supply in a not so far off future. An energy crisis is inevitable with no other energy sources readily available. Cities will fall and society will crumble in its wake. This is the story of four individuals' journey through a war begotten through this disaster. Their lives, as well as everyone else's, will never be the same once all is said and done.
